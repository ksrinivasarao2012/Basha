Artificial intelligence is transforming how we interact with technology; indeed, machines can now translate text and generate natural-sounding speech across many languages. This project, Basha, is designed to demonstrate how an end-to-end Text-to-Speech system works. It splits long-form text on clause boundaries, translates the segments into a target language, synthesizes individual audio clips, and stitches them back together with natural pauses. If you are reading this, the system is working perfectly. Let's translate this into Telugu, Hindi, German, or Spanish to test it out!
